After a recent firmware update, my aircraft won't enter Return-to-Home when I trigger it from the controller. It just hovers in place. What should I check?

Return-to-Home is likely disabled because the new firmware reset your Return-to-Home altitude threshold to the factory default, which may be above your current AGL.

Likely cause: The firmware update reverts RTH settings unless you migrate your config profile before updating.

Check: Open the IF Pilot app → Aircraft Settings → Safety → confirm Return-to-Home is enabled and the minimum altitude is set below your operating height.

Safety: Do not force-land or override geofence limits to test RTH. Verify prop clearance and airspace before initiating any automated return sequence.
Source: docs.inspiredflight.com/if1200/return-to-home
